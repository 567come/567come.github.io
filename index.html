Project Highlights

High Sensitivity

Our microbial sensor can detect microplastics as low as 0.1 mg/L, realizing rapid and accurate visual detection without complex equipment.

Green & Efficient

Engineered strains degrade microplastics into harmless small-molecule substances, avoiding secondary pollution and reducing treatment costs.

Integrated System

Combining detection and degradation in one system, providing a one-stop solution for environmental microplastic pollution control.

Our Project Overview

Microplastic Detection

We construct engineered microbial sensors that can visually detect microplastics in water samples with high sensitivity.

Biodegradation

We screen and engineer microorganisms to efficiently degrade microplastics, providing a green and low-cost solution.

Environmental Impact

Our integrated system aims to mitigate microplastic pollution, protecting aquatic ecosystems and human health.
